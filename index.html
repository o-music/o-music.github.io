George Zhao

George is a musician and a instrumental music teacher.

About

Qualifications

George is a graduate of Shenyang Conservatory of Music from China, and is a PhD candidate at Rhodes University in South Africa.

ElectroAcoustic Music

George is a musician who creates and performs electroacoustic music. He was honored to have performed at Musicacoustica-Beijing Festival and Shanghai International Electroacoustic Music Week in China. He also had the privilege of performing at Canberra International Music Festival, National Multicultural Festival, and National Science Week in Australia, as well as performances at universities in the UK and US.

Tuition

George is a instrumental music teacher who teaches students playing the piano and accordion, and deliveries comprehensive music education via private instrumental music tuition. He believes that learning music helps students to establish a positive character and a healthy self-image which will accompany them in their lifetime. He strives to be an inspiring music teacher and to bring happiness through music to his students.

George can be reached by email at george@omusic.au.
